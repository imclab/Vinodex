Wine + Index Your Wines, Managed

Vinodex makes managing your wine collection a snap! Want to organize your collection of wines without breaking a sweat? With Vinodex, you can organize your wines by vintage, winery, grape varietal, or even the real-time market price.

Autocomplete 1, 2, 3, Cheese!

Purchased a new wine recently? Be sure to add that wine to your virtual wine collection. Vinodex makes this easy for you. All you have to do is take a picture of the label of your newly purchased wine or type in its name. A picture of its barcode will work too!

Cellars Put a Location on It

Want to impress your hot date but having trouble locating a particular wine in your collection? Simply log in to Vinodex and search for that wine you know will impress. Vinodex lets you organized your wines into cellars, so you'll know where exactly you stored it and spend more time impressing your significant other.

Sommelier At Your Service

Wish you knew what wine to pair with a given meal, or vice versa? Tingle your taste buds. Give the Sommelier feature a try. Just input the type of meal you will be eating and Vinodex will tell you which of your wines will pair best with your meal.

Hard Copies

Rather have a physical copy of your wine collection? Maybe you want to show off your wine collection to guests without having to turn on a computer? Vinodex provides you with the option to print a catalog of your wine collection!

Market Price

Interested in the market price of a given wine? Vinodex provides the market price of any wine. Simply type in the wine you are interested in purchasing and Vinodex will provide you with the current market price.
